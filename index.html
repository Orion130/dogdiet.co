Welcome to Dog Diet!

Dog diet is a website that helps your dog have a healthy diet by recognizing its weight and calorie intake. Based off these categories we establish the recommended food brands and portions for your furry or fur less friend !!!

Find out your dogs diet with our dog quiz!

Dog Quiz

Small Dogs

Small dogs can range from 11-22Ibs, and some of their breeds consist of Pomeranians, Chihuahuas, and Pugs. They typically consume 139- 233 calories a day.

*Image of a Pomeranian

Medium Dogs

Medium dogs can range from 22-55lbs, and some of their breeds consist of Airedale Terriers, American Foxhounds, and American Water Spaniels. They typically consume 342-576 calories a day.

*Image of a Laberdoodle

Large Dogs

Large dogs can range from 55-99lbs, and some of their breeds consist of Bernese Mountain Dogs, Labrador Retrievers, and German Shepherds. They typically consume 781-1145 calories a day.

*Image of a Australian Shepherd

Very Large Dogs

Very Large dogs can weigh over 99lbs, and some of their breeds consist of Great Danes, Newfoundlands, and Cane Corsos. They typically consume 1313-1926 calories a day.

About Us
Our Mission

Our mission is to address dog owners' need for knowing what to feed their dogs in order to keep them healthy. Through our website we help to make it easy for people to get recommendations for what to feed their dogs. Dogs and their owners both benefit from this development without the worry and hassle of visiting a Veterinarian. It also allows the owners to have peace of mind when knowing their dogs are healthy. Dog owners may need this info because their dog could be facing dietary issues if given the wrong food or incorrect amount of food for their breed.
